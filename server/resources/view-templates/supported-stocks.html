Make Some Money Stocks

Supported Stocks

The machine learning models are trained on stock data from all S&P 500 companies. Notable companies include the following:

  • Apple Inc. (AAPL)
  • Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD)
  • Amazon.com, Inc. (AMZN)
  • Microsoft Corporation (MSFT)
  • NVDIA Corporation (NVDA)

For a full list of stocks that the machine learning models are trained on, click here.

The model uses the open, high, low, and close prices along with the volume of companies' stocks to predict the closing price on a future day.
